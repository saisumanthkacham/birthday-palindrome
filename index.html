Check out if your Birthdate is Palindrome .

A Palindrome is a Word/number which reads the same backward as forward

Enter your Birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a Palindrome

This app check your birthdate in 6 formats dd-mm-yyyy , mm-dd-yyyy,yyyy- mm-dd, dd-mm-yy,mm-dd-yy,yy- mm-dd like if your bdate is 01 sep 1994 then app will check for 01091994 ,09011994,19940901, 010995,090195,950901.
